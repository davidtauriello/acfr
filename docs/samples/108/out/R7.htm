- Definition

Other deferred inflows of resources represents an acquisition of net position by the government that is applicable to a future reporting period. Used for amounts on the governmental funds statements, reported on a modified accrual basis. Must be used with a typed dimension to indicate a fact which is custom created for that reporting entity.
