- Definition

Employer’s required contributions to the pension plan for the reporting period and separate liabilities to the pension plan that arise in the reporting period. This is the difference in net pension liability from the previous measurement date to the current measurement date. Pension expense includes the current period service cost, interest on the total pension liability, changes in benefit terms that are required to be recognized immediately, projected earnings on the pension plan investments and the current period’s recognition of deferred outflows and inflows.
